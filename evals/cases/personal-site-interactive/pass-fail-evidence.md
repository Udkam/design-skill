# Pass / Fail Evidence

Pass when:

- The first viewport says personal lab, portfolio, or individual maker surface through copy and structure.
- Route dock entries stay visible without replacing project evidence.
- Pet/floating controls have 44px or larger targets and mobile-safe offsets.
- `requestAnimationFrame` is cancelled or never started when reduced motion is active.
- Shared interactive islands are called out for blast-radius review.

Fail when:

- The page becomes a purple/blue SaaS hero with generic feature cards.
- Pet, console, repo, or game identity is removed without replacement.
- CSS reduced-motion exists but JS loops, video, canvas, or timelines keep running.
- Floating controls overlap the dock or primary mobile content.
