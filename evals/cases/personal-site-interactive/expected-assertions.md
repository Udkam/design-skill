# Expected Assertions

- First viewport reads as a personal lab or portfolio, not a SaaS product page.
- Projects, Game, Repo, and Console routes are visible in navigation or dock affordances.
- The pet/toy control is present, bounded, optional, and does not cover primary content.
- Touch targets for dock and pet controls are at least 44px.
- JS animation uses `matchMedia("(prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)")` and cancels `requestAnimationFrame`.
- Loading, empty, error, disabled, selected, pending, and success states are represented.
- Shared island changes require a blast-radius note in the human review.
